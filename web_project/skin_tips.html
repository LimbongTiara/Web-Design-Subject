Common Skin Problems & Solutions

üî¥ Acne & Breakouts

Causes: Excess oil, clogged pores, bacteria

Solutions:

  • Use salicylic acid or benzoyl peroxide
  • Don't pick or squeeze pimples
  • Keep hair and hands away from face
  • Change pillowcases regularly

üü§ Dark Spots

Causes: Sun damage, acne scars, aging

Solutions:

  • Use vitamin C serum daily
  • Apply sunscreen religiously
  • Try niacinamide products
  • Consider gentle exfoliation

üëÄ Under Eye Bags

Causes: Lack of sleep, dehydration, genetics

Solutions:

  • Get 7-8 hours of sleep
  • Use eye cream with caffeine
  • Apply cold compress
  • Stay hydrated
